Admission

Our admission process is designed to welcome aspiring musicians of all levels, providing them with the opportunity to grow their skills in a supportive and inspiring environment.
  • Minimum age limit for enrollment is 5 years and above.
  • A student is initially allowed to take only one main instrument but if he/she is a quick learner​, they are allowed to approach their respective teachers+parents and talk to school admin after, to undertake more than one instrument.
  • All fees must be paid either by the first week or the end of each month, so that we can smoothly process faculties' salaries and run the school without any hassle. Your cooperation is much appreciated.
  • While Gpay-ing, parents are kindly requested to add their children's name and month.

Rules for  Recovery Classes:

  1. Recovery classes are given only if parents inform 2-3 days ahead with valid reasons.
  2. If teachers are absent due to their performances then they are told to arrange substitute or make schedule to give students' recovery classes for that day. So, parents need not worry as the admin will handle this.
  3. No absentees unless students are seriously ill, as music class per student is just one week. Kindly, take it seriously and treasure it. It is for the student's benefit.
  4. If students suddenly takes break for 2-3 months and returns to take classes @ Kinimi School of Music - ₹1000 for booking their slot.
  5. If consecutively absent for 2-3 classes, teachers will be able to give only one class as it is difficult to arrange and make time for multiple recovery classes. Remember! All are busy but kindly prioritize music class as again, it is only once a week!
  6. Sudden cancellation of classes will be charged ₹300 fine per class.

Rules and Regulations

  1. The Academic year is from January to December
  2. First Semester - January to June and Second Semester - July to December. There will be summer, Christmas and New Year break.
  3. Working hours - 11 am to 7 pm
  4. Keep the school clean, kindly throw all the dirt in the dustbin.
  5. Every Students will have a 45 minutes individual lessons once a week whether group or individual classes, but during recitals or special events performances, group or individual classes may go for more than an hour
  6. Crash course is allowed for a month, 6 months or a year.
  7. Twice a week classes will be taken under special consideration and valid reasons.
  8. Students' recitals will be conducted every twice a year to allow parents to see children's progress and to support them in music.
  9. Students absent for one whole term (6 months) will not be allowed to continue their studies in the school. If there are special reasons, highlight it to the admin. There will be reconsideration under valid reasons. This is for the school to function smoothly.

Kinimi music school is a Christ-centered academy. Our school will not only teach music but we will also look into the spiritual and psychological needs of our students.
